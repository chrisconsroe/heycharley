My Story

I received my bachelor's degree in Business Management from Stetson University in 2011 and am currently enrolled in my Masters program at Columbia University in New York. I've also spent ten years learning the insurance industry firsthand—working my way from 'envelope sealer' to CEO.

I launched my first company during my senior year at Stetson—a software startup that published multiple applications featured by iTunes Editors and reached Top 25 in downloads. I also started a small production company and co-founded an e-commerce agriculture business.

My associates and I embrace three key words when it comes to consulting: "I don’t know". We are curious, free thinking individuals that are motivated by creatively solving challenging problems. We believe it starts with admitting what we don’t know and asking every client the right questions.

I'm obsessed with brilliant storytelling—whether it comes from Netflix or classic novels. I'm an avid sports fan and like to hit the public basketball courts in NYC on the weekends (while trying not to get dunked on). I love to walk my dog through the park and am always ready to travel to somewhere I've never been.
